# Keep creative content out of the account backoffice

The initial backoffice will expose account identity, tiers, generation counts,
provider metadata, token usage, costs, and failures, but not prompts, shader
code, generated assets, or project documents. Account administration and cost
investigation do not require routine access to Creative Content, and adding it
would create a larger privacy and authorization surface. Any future diagnostic
content access must be designed as a separate audited capability.
