# v0.44 Artifact Product Surfaces

Status: planned. Blocked by the v0.42 release gate and may be implemented in
parallel with v0.43 after that frontier. Delivery is tracked in GitHub milestone
[`v0.44 Artifact Product Surfaces`](https://github.com/shchilkin/artifact/milestone/3).

## Purpose

v0.44 migrates every non-editor Artifact product route onto the Artifact Design
System and removes the leaked internal shader-debug route.

The release thesis is: Artifact presents one coherent product and learning
surface from public discovery through Projects and Docs.

## Scope

- Close the non-editor Artifact surface inventory.
- Migrate the public shell, recovery/error states, home, showcase, examples,
  Projects, Docs, recipes, reference routes, and Nodes/effects learning surface.
- Remove `/debug/shaders`, its route registration, and standalone CSS.

## Non-Goals

- No editor workflow, inspector, canvas, renderer, or graph migration.
- No auth, project persistence, starter-document, shader, or render behavior
  change.
- The Artifact AI Generation composer remains the v0.42 proof-consumer scope.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every non-editor product route is migrated or an approved non-goal.
- Navigation, recovery, discovery, Projects, and Docs retain their existing
  route, auth, persistence, and starter behavior.
- `/debug/shaders` and its route-specific UI code are absent.
- Desktop/mobile focus, overflow, hierarchy, and route-state conformance pass.
